Common Questions

How do I change recording quality?

Go to the Settings Tab > Audio Quality to switch between WAV and M4A.

Where are my files backed up?

Enable iCloud, Google Drive, or Dropbox in Settings > Cloud Sync.

How does Voice Slate work?

Tap the mic icon next to the FX Name field to dictate via on-device speech-to-text.

How do I export my recordings?

Select files, then tap the Share icon. In Settings, you can also export a ZIP archive with audio and metadata.
