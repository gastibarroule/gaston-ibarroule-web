Documentation

Everything you need to know about setting up and using Sonidata for professional UCS field recording.

🎙️

Recording

Sonidata gives you full control over your recording format with three independent settings in **Settings → Audio**:

🏷️

Tagging & Metadata (The Naming View)

The Naming View is the core of Sonidata. It's where you assign UCS-compliant metadata to your recordings.

🎤

Voice Slate

Voice Slate lets you **dictate the FX Name** using speech-to-text instead of typing.

🤖

Siri Shortcuts & Automation

Control Sonidata hands-free with **Siri, the Shortcuts app, Back Tap, or the Action Button**.

📍

Location Tagging

Sonidata can automatically tag recordings with GPS location data.

📚

Browsing Your Library

Sonidata offers **four ways** to browse your recordings:

📁

Folder Organization

Sonidata can automatically organize your recordings into a UCS folder structure.

📸

Photo Attachments

You can attach a photo to any recording — useful for documenting the recording environment, location, or source.

📦
